Privacy Policy

Suncoast Pros QuickBooks Integration

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Suncoast Pros ("we," "our," or "the Company") operates a QuickBooks Online integration application ("the App") for internal business use. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information accessed through the App.

2. Information We Access

Through the Intuit QuickBooks Online API, the App may access the following types of business data:

  • Customer and vendor information (names, contact details, billing addresses)
  • Financial records (invoices, estimates, bills, payments, journal entries)
  • Product and service listings
  • Employee information
  • Financial reports (balance sheet, profit & loss, cash flow)
  • Chart of accounts

3. How We Use Information

All data accessed through the App is used solely for:

  • Managing day-to-day accounting operations for Suncoast Pros
  • Generating financial reports for internal business use
  • Managing customer and vendor relationships
  • Invoicing and payment tracking

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with third parties. Data accessed through the App is used exclusively for internal Suncoast Pros business operations.

5. Data Storage and Security

The App operates on secure, access-controlled servers. QuickBooks data is accessed in real-time through Intuit's official APIs and is not permanently stored outside of QuickBooks Online. Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted at rest.

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain independent copies of QuickBooks data. All financial data remains within the QuickBooks Online platform. API access tokens are retained only as long as necessary for the App to function and are revoked upon disconnection.

7. Your Rights

As the QuickBooks account owner, you may at any time:

  • Revoke the App's access to your QuickBooks data through your Intuit account settings
  • Request information about what data the App has accessed
  • Request deletion of any locally cached data

8. Intuit Platform Compliance

This App complies with Intuit's platform policies and developer terms of service. We use only officially supported QuickBooks Online APIs and adhere to Intuit's data handling requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
